Project Overview

BearEdvance is an AI-powered educational technology platform that revolutionizes how educators create video content. By leveraging cutting-edge AI technologies including OpenAI's GPT for script generation, Microsoft Azure's text-to-speech services, and Wav2Lip for realistic video synthesis, the platform transforms a simple topic input into a complete educational video in under 5 minutes.

The Challenge

Educators spend 4-6 hours creating a single lecture video, involving scriptwriting, slide design, recording, and editing. This time-intensive process limits content creation and prevents educators from focusing on direct student interaction and personalized teaching.

My Solution

As Product Design Manager, I led the design of an end-to-end automated platform that takes a topic input and generates professional educational videos through an AI pipeline. The interface prioritizes simplicity and transparency, making complex AI accessible to non-technical educators.

Impact

The platform reduced video creation time from 4-6 hours to 5 minutes (80% workload reduction), improved task completion rates by 22%, and enabled educators to produce 10x more content while maintaining quality and pedagogical effectiveness.

Research & Discovery

UNDERSTANDING EDUCATOR PAIN POINTS

Through interviews with 15+ educators and observation of their video creation workflows, I identified critical pain points that informed our design strategy. Educators consistently reported spending 4-6 hours per video on tasks that could be automated.

TIME CONSTRAINTS

  • Scriptwriting takes 1-2 hours per video
  • Slide design requires 1-1.5 hours
  • Recording and re-recording takes 1-2 hours
  • Editing and post-production adds 1-1.5 hours

TECHNICAL BARRIERS

  • Steep learning curve for video editing software
  • Expensive equipment and software licenses
  • Lack of design skills for professional-looking slides
  • Difficulty maintaining consistency across videos

CONTENT CHALLENGES

  • Difficulty structuring complex topics for video format
  • Struggle to balance depth and accessibility
  • Need for multiple iterations to get tone right
  • Limited ability to personalize for different audiences

Key Insight: Educators don't want to become video producers—they want to focus on teaching. The ideal solution would automate the technical aspects of video creation while preserving pedagogical control and content quality.

Technical Workflow & AI PIPELINE

One of my key design challenges was making a complex AI pipeline feel simple and trustworthy. The platform orchestrates multiple AI services in sequence, and I designed the interface to provide transparency at each step while hiding unnecessary technical complexity.

The 4-Step AI Pipeline

📝

Step 1: Topic Input

Educator enters topic and selects content type

🤖

Step 2: Script Generation

OpenAI GPT-3.5 generates educational script

🎙️

Step 3: Audio Synthesis

🎬

Design Decision: I designed a transparent progress dashboard that shows each pipeline step in real-time. This builds trust by demystifying the AI process and manages expectations during the 2-3 minute generation time. Users see exactly what's happening at each stage.

Figma Wireframes & UI Design

Below are the complete set of wireframes I designed in Figma, covering the entire user journey from authentication to content creation and discovery. Each wireframe demonstrates thoughtful information architecture, user-centered design, and attention to interaction patterns.

Dashboard: Central Hub for Educators

The dashboard serves as the central hub, providing quick access to courses, progress tracking, AI video creation, and notifications. Clear navigation and information hierarchy enable educators to efficiently manage their teaching activities.

AI Video Creation Interface: Simplified Multi-Step Workflow

The video creation interface breaks down the complex AI pipeline into manageable steps: script input, AI voice selection, visual asset upload, and preview. This step-by-step approach guides educators through the process while providing transparency and control.

Course Content Page: Content-First Layout

The course page prioritizes video content with a large player, while providing contextual information through lesson lists, course descriptions, and comment sections. This layout supports both content consumption and community engagement.

Course Discovery: Search and Browse Experience

The discovery page features prominent search functionality and category filters, allowing educators to efficiently find relevant courses. The grid layout provides visual browsing while maintaining information density and scalability.

Authentication: Streamlined Login & Signup

The authentication screen offers multiple login options (email/password, Google, Apple, Facebook) to accommodate diverse user preferences and reduce friction during onboarding. The minimalist design focuses on essential elements.

User Profile & Settings: Organized Information Management

The profile page organizes settings into clear categories (Personal Information, Account Settings, Notifications, Subscription), empowering users with control over their experience while maintaining visual consistency.

Design System Approach: All wireframes follow a consistent design language with reusable components, standardized spacing, and clear visual hierarchy. This systematic approach ensures scalability and maintainability as the platform grows.

Key Learnings

1. TRANSPARENCY BUILDS TRUST

Showing the AI pipeline in real-time (script generation → audio synthesis → video assembly) significantly increased user trust. Educators appreciated seeing "behind the curtain" rather than treating the platform as a black box.

2. CONTROL IS ESSENTIAL

While automation saves time, educators need control over final output. Adding script editing and character selection features increased adoption by 40% compared to our initial fully-automated prototype.

3. QUALITY OVER SPEED

Early testing revealed that educators would rather wait 5 minutes for high-quality output than get instant low-quality results. We optimized for quality while keeping generation time under 5 minutes.
